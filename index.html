More about me

Hey there! My name is Marc Anthony. Easy to remember, it’s just like the singer.

I’m a product guy who is passionate about startups, product design, Lean Startup methods, emerging tech, and personal growth.

I grew up in Southwest Florida, in a town called Naples. After graduating from the University of Notre Dame, I lived in Charlotte NC for a year before moving to Chicago.

Most recently, I’m Product Creator at Buffer, where we build tools for social media pros and web entrepreneurs. This is an amazing opportunity to delight over two million users with Lean iterations and the latest web technologies.

Right before Buffer, I was a Product Manager at Dose Media, where we built viral products like Dose.com, OMG Facts, and Blanks.

Previously, I founded a social events startup Cannonball, and operated a delivery company called DormDrinks while in college.
