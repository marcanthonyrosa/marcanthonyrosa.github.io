Hey there! My name is Marc Anthony. Easy to remember, it’s just like the singer (or Roman triumverate).

A little about myself: I grew up in Southwest Florida, in a town called Naples. After graduating from the University of Notre Dame, I lived in Chicago for a few years, and then recently made my way to Houston.

I'm a Product Manager for technology companies. I'm also learning front-end web development in my spare time.

Most recently, I was a Product Creator at Buffer, where I helped create social media management tools for 3+ million users. I had the opportunity to help iterate the core product, as well as launch Buffer's first product acquisition, Respond by Buffer.

Previously, I was Product Manager at Dose Media, where we built viral products like Dose.com, OMG Facts, and Blanks.

Earlier in my career, I founded a social events startup Cannonball, and owned/operated a delivery company called DormDrinks while in college. These two were my first forays into the startup world.

I love to design, validate, and build the latest products. I get excited about solving problems for users, and helping design and build those experiences!
